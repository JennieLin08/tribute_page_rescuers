TO ALL RESCUERS

"Honoring Courage, Celebrating Sacrifice."

“They showed bravery and heroism in facing danger despite the perils to their own lives to fulfill their duty of saving other people,”

“I'm extremely grateful to all the first responders, veterans and their families for all their sacrifices. -Taya Kyle”

HEROIC RESCUE | On July 24, 2024, the 525th Combat Engineer “Forerunner” Battalion of the Combat Engineer Regiment, Philippine Army, conducted ongoing search and rescue operations across Metro Manila. Deploying three Light Urban Search and Rescue (USAR) Teams, they successfully rescued approximately 500 individuals in a single day. Team Forerunner tirelessly collaborates with local government units to locate and save residents affected by the widespread flooding. Their swift and effective response highlights the dedication and professionalism of the unit in Metro Manila's search and rescue operations. Source: Read more...
